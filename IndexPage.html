About Us

iControlTech Inc. offers various services from the design, supply, installation, testing, commissioning, and preventive maintenance services for Automation and Power/Energy Management systems.

We provide cost-effective system integration services based on the client's requirements. The company may assist in project conceptualization, e.g. evaluation of the existing system and re-designing of an enhanced integrated control system. We have completed several projects in Power Management and Automation solutions for critical infrastructure like airports, power generation plants, and water distribution facilities as well as Energy Savings solutions for hotels, offices, and commercial buildings.

The company started as a sole proprietorship under iControlTech Electrical and Industrial Services in January 2013. It was established and managed by Engr. Freddie Catacutan who has more than 30 years of experience in the field of Electrical, Instrumentation & Control Systems Engineering. As the business aims to expand, the company incorporates in May 2018. The company has established partnership with the leaders in Drives, Automation and Digital Energy.

★ Certified Alliance System Integrator Partner of Schneider Electric for Automation, SCADA System & Power Management System.

★ Authorized System Integrator & Service Partner of Danfoss-Vacon Power Conversion units for Hybrid Electrification solution.

★ Authorized Service Partner of Control Techniques (NIDEC) Drives.

★ Authorized partner of NARADA for Battery Energy Storage System.

We specialize in Redundant & Hot Standby Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) & SCADA systems for mission-critical infrastructure (like airports, power & utilities), Power Metering and Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) system, Variable Frequency Drives (VFD), Grid Converters, Human-Machine Interface (HMI), Instrumentation & Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) for TeleControl Applications.

The company employs experienced and certified System Engineers, who provide 24/7 technical support. iControlTech is a licensed contractor under the Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board (PCAB) and holds a Platinum Membership under Philippine Government Electronic Procurement System (PhilGEPS).
